About

Designing with reasoning, not ritual

I'm a UX/UI product designer who believes the strongest portfolios lead with design decisions and their trade-offs — not a linear walk through Research → Persona → Journey Map → Final Design.

Every case study here follows the Decisive One Page structure: Problem → Hypothesis → Evidence → Design Decision with trade-offs → Outcome. If you can't find the insight in 30 seconds, the page failed.

Currently exploring vibe coding, AI-assisted design tools, and the intersection of research rigor with rapid prototyping.
