About

Designing with reasoning, not ritual

I believe a strong design is measured by the clarity of its decisions, not the volume of its deliverables. Every project I take on follows the same discipline: define the real problem, gather evidence, make explicit trade-offs, and measure what changed.

My current focus is agentic AI — exploring how autonomous systems reshape research, prototyping, and the designer's role from delivering fixed solutions to shaping systems that evolve with people over time.

Open to collaboration across thoughtful, forward-thinking projects.
