03 — Case Study

Makahealth: Build patient Portal

Makahealth is a wellness-focused startup where practitioners and patients connect through integrated personal health data. The platform enables communication and delivers science-backed recommendations, vetted by leading medical professionals, to help users optimize their health.

It brings together a functional wellness questionnaire, a personal data vault, and a curated marketplace—creating a connected ecosystem where users can better understand and manage their well-being.

Design constraints

First and only designer on the team

I'm the first and only UX/UI designer to join Makahealth's design team. The previous design work was led by a project manager. As the sole designer, I also had to conduct questionnaires, data analysis, competitive analysis, and participate in stakeholder meetings — all while learning how to navigate a real product team for the first time.

Design constraints overview
Solutions

The all-in-one health data view

I'm not a business founder, but as a member of "The Blue" — the design team — I was able to get directly in front of users. I tried to approach UX/UI design in a modern "The Blue" and prioritized the all-in-one health data view so users could see everything in one place.

Scope and timeline

Bi-weekly syncs, one-month sprint

Our design team regularly met every Bi-weekly for internal alignment. Stakeholders to handoff their sensitive project in one full-throttle manner the committed task with decisions and accountability.

Research

Research questionnaire

The objective for our research survey was to make an accessible Google questionnaire that covered all age group distributions. I managed to collect 89 survey participants.

Question 1
Do you regularly track your health data?
Question 2
What health tools or apps do you currently use?
Question 3
What frustrations do you experience with current health portals?
Question 4
What would make you trust a digital health platform?

Synthesis

Synthesizing research data

Based on the completed fieldwork and the design team's direction, I led the data analysis using affinity mapping to sort and cluster the survey responses into research findings.
